## Overview

InstaDP lets you view and download Instagram profiles and pictures in their original high-definition size without logging in. You access public profiles easily through a simple web interface. This service prioritizes privacy and speed, delivering content directly without third-party trackers.

## Quick Start

You begin by entering an Instagram username in the search bar on the homepage. InstaDP fetches the profile data in seconds, displaying photos in full resolution. Download individual images or the entire profile gallery with one click.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search Profile" icon="search">
    Type the username, like `exampleuser`, and hit enter.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Content" icon="eye">
    Scroll through posts and stories in HD quality.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download Files" icon="download">
    Select images and save them to your device.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  For best results, use public profiles only, as private accounts require login which InstaDP avoids for privacy.
</Callout>

## Why Choose InstaDP?

You benefit from fast loading times under `<1` second for most profiles. The platform supports batch downloads, allowing you to save multiple images at once. Unlike other tools, InstaDP does not store your data or require sign-ups.

  Integrate InstaDP with scripts for automated downloads. Use the API endpoints to fetch profile data programmatically.

  ```javascript
  fetch('https://api.instadp.com/profiles/exampleuser')
    .then(response => response.json())
    .then(data => console.log(data.images));
  ```

## Security and Privacy

InstaDP encrypts all transfers to protect content. You never expose credentials, as the service operates on public data only. Regular updates ensure compliance with Instagram's terms.
